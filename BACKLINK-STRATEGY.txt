## 7. LOCAL CONTENT IDEAS (To EARN backlinks naturally)
# ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
These content ideas can attract links from Kerala parenting blogs,
local news, and community pages without spending money:

  a) "Best Kids Cycles for Kerala Roads" — blog post or Facebook Note
     → Mention types of cycles best suited for Kerala's road conditions
     → Parents groups and parenting blogs may share it

  b) "Electric Ride-On Toys: What to Look For" — WhatsApp-shareable image
     → Post on your Facebook page; parenting communities will reshare

  c) "Kottarakara Cycle Guide" — simple page or post
     → Explains cycling paths, cycling events, local interest
     → Kerala cycling communities may link to it

  d) Back-to-school cycle promotion (August every year)
     → Kerala school term start is a natural buying trigger
     → Run a promotion and post to local community Facebook groups

  e) Donate a cycle to a local school or charity (PR idea)
     → Local news outlets and community pages may cover this naturally

## 9. REVIEW STRATEGY
# ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Reviews are not backlinks but they ARE a local ranking signal:

  - After every sale, share your Google Maps review link with the customer
  - Aim for 20+ Google reviews within 3 months
  - Respond to all reviews (Google rewards active listings)
  - Ask happy WhatsApp customers to paste their review on Google

Your Google review link will look like:
  https://g.page/r/YOUR_PLACE_ID/review
  (Get this from your Google Business Profile dashboard)
